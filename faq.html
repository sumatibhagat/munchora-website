What is Makhana?

Makhana, also known as fox nuts or lotus seeds, is a traditional Indian superfood rich in nutrients. It has been part of Ayurveda and household kitchens for centuries.

Why is Makhana considered a superfood?

Makhana is rich in antioxidants, great for digestion, supports heart health, and even helps with weight management. It's gluten-free and packed with anti-aging properties too!

Is Zesty Makhana safe for kids?

Yes! It's a smart tiffin snack—even toddlers love the crunch. Just ensure they’re supervised while munching.

How should I store the jars?

Our jars are sealed for freshness. After opening, keep the lid tightly shut and store in a cool, dry place. No pouch to fuss with!

What’s the shelf life of Zesty Makhana?

Each jar stays fresh for 4 months. There are no added preservatives or MSG—just clean ingredients sealed for a crunch that lasts.

My Makhana isn’t crunchy anymore. What can I do?

No worries! Just microwave for 30–40 seconds and the crispiness will bounce right back.
