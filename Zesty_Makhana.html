Smart storage tips

Each jar comes sealed to keep your Makhana fresh and crunchy. Once opened, keep the lid tightly closed and store in a cool, dry place. If left open for long, just microwave for 30–40 seconds to bring the crunch right back!

Why Makhana is worth munching?

Makhana, also called fox nuts or lotus seeds, is a superfood loved for centuries. India is the world’s largest producer—especially regions like Bihar and Madhubani. But now, it’s winning hearts worldwide for a reason:

🌿 Rich in antioxidants for a youthful glow
🧠 Supports brain health and cognitive function
💪 Boosts bone strength and joint health
❤️ Great for the heart and blood sugar control
🔥 Aids digestion and weight management
